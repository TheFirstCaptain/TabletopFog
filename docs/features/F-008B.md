# F-008B: Fullscreen Player View

## Objective

Let the Player Display enter and exit browser fullscreen from the player page.

## User Value

The GM can put the player-facing map on a TV or tablet with less browser chrome
during play.

## Non-Goals

- Do not add GM-side fullscreen controls.
- Do not add server APIs, Socket.IO messages, or persistence for fullscreen
  state.
- Do not change what map, fog, or encounter is shown to players.
- Do not expose GM controls on the Player Display.
- Do not add QR code generation or player URL discovery.
- Do not require fullscreen to be synchronized across player devices.

## Acceptance Notes

- The Player Display shows a compact `Enter fullscreen` control in the player
  status bar.
- Activating the control requests browser fullscreen for the Player Display.
- When fullscreen is active, the control changes to `Exit fullscreen`.
- If fullscreen is unavailable or denied, the Player Display remains usable and
  shows quiet player-local status text.
- Fullscreen state is local to that browser and does not mutate GM state,
  campaign state, map/fog state, or other Player Displays.
